Finance Policies of Tamil Nadu

Explore the financial initiatives and support programs

Pudhumai Penn Scheme

Women's Education Empowerment

A welfare scheme launched by the Tamil Nadu government to support women's education and empowerment.

  • Financial assistance for higher education
  • Aims to reduce dropout rates among girl students
  • Provides support for professional and vocational courses
  • Encourages women's educational advancement
Tamilar Pudhalvan Scheme

Skill Development Program

A comprehensive skill development program aimed at enhancing employability of youth in Tamil Nadu.

  • Free skill training programs
  • Focus on industry-relevant skills
  • Provides employment opportunities
  • Bridges skill gaps in the job market
